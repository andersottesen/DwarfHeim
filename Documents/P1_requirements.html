Client

Client Name: DwarfHeim
Contact Person: Hans Kleven

Our client is a Norwegian game development company, introduced by one of our team members; Eirik Fosse. The client is in the process of making a new real time pc strategy game called DwarfHeim, and wants a new website to promote their game, and to attract possible investors and collaborators. DwarfHeim will feature an innovative gameplay experience centered around cooperation and asymmetrical play styles, that are cartoony and based on humour.

Meeting with the client

On September 3rd we met three of the DwarHeim team members for a meeting about our potential creation of a website for them. We agreed quite quickly to do the project with them and spent the rest of the time discussing requirements and design. They told us about their game, how it will look, how they want it to feel and we discussed the constraints of our project. We arranged that they will provide conceptual art, FAQ and other information about the site and that they will come in and review our design document.

Purpose and Goals

The purpose of the website is to give gamers and investors information and news about DwarfHeim. This website will be a place where gamers can get updates and status about the game in development, and a place where investors can read about the team of developers and get in contact with them. The main purpose is to get the gamers interested in playing the game. By adding videos and concept art from the game to the web page gamers will get to know the game and hopefully be motivated to play it. Simultaneously this will show the investors what the game is about and get them interested in the development team. A secondary purpose of the website is to inform stakeholders (gamers and investors) about the development team. This will include a brief presentation of the team members and their position in the DwarfHeim project. The goal of the web page is that it should be easy to navigate and find relevant information for both gamers and investors. The site should also be minimalistic, which means that the web page should be should not be clean and bright, and no any disturbing elements.

Audience

The main target audience of this web page is meant to be potential gamers. We also aim at attracting potential investors. For the gamers we will present trailers, conceptual art and information about the game to get them interested and motivated to play the game. Investors on the other hand will want to find information about the developers, technical information, the business model and contact information. For both gamers and investors we will include interviews with all the team members so that the stakeholders can get to know the team better.

Content of the Website

  • Landing page
  • The landing page’s main goal is to catch the attention of the visitors. This page will contain a video trailer of the game, pictures, short introduction text and links the visitor further into the page.
  • Homepage
  • This is the main page and will contain images and text that shows the visitors what kind of information that can be found on the website and where the visitor should go next.
  • Conceptual art
  • This page will contain a gallery with pictures and videos of conceptual art from the game.
  • Contact us
  • On this page the visitor can find information about how to contact the members of the DwarHeim team.There will also be contact form for general questions that will be sent to DwarfHeim’s email account.
  • FAQ
  • Common questions will be answered on this page. Questions and answers should be organized into relevant categories.
  • Investors' page
  • When possible investors and other potential collaborators visit the site, they will find relevant information here. This will feature business related information, and technical notes.
  • About the crew
  • This page will contain information about the team members of DwarHeim. They will be presented in the form of an interview with pictures. These interviews will also contain contact information.
  • About DwarfHeim
  • Here the visitor will be able to read about the project itself, what the game is about, and some of the development process.

Technical Challenges

  • Since we are planning to use videos and images the website might load slowly.
  • Since we are running the website on a web-server, we can meet some challenges concerning getting everything to run as planned.
  • One of our goals is to make the site run on all types of devices, which means it has to be responsive and will acquire some work and testing.
